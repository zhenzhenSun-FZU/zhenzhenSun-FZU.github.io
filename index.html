About me

I am currently an assistant professor at Fuzhou University. I've received the bachelor, master and Ph.D. degree (supervised by Prof. Jihua Zhu) from Xi'an Jiaotong University from 2015, 2018, and 2021, respectively. I joined the College of Computer and Data Science of Fuzhou University in 2022.

My research area is machine learning and data mining, with special interests in multi-view learning, label distribution learning, and subspace clustering.

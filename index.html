About me

I am currently a lecturer at Huaqiao University. I've received the bachelor and Ph.D. degree (supervised by Prof. Yuanlong Yu) from Fuzhou University from 2015 and 2021, respectively. I joined the College of Computer Science and Tonology of Huaqiao University in July 2021.

My research area is machine learning and data mining, with special interests in multi-label learning, partial multi-label learning, and feature selection.
